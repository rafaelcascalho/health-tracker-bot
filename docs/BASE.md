# Baseline

Goals:

1. Loose weight
2. Gain muscle and strength

Assumptions:

- diet already exists (made by nutritionist)
- gym training schedule already exists (made by professional personal trainer)

## Blockers (unmovable)

- 8h am to 6h pm monday trough friday (work)
- ignore wildcard times like transportation time from location to pilates classes, or even between specific events
  - assume I'll figure those out without required placeholders in the scheduled time of activities

### Water

- 3.6 L a day with
  - 1.2 L bottle (default)
  - 0.6 L bottle
- must finish before 8 pm everyday

## Sleep

- do EOD chores at 9h30 (check if I have chores for the day)
- go to the bedroom at 10pm
  - shower, brush teeth, respond messages, etc before going to bed
- be in bad 10h30 pm (do NOT use phone in bed)
- wake up at 7h am

## Meals

- 8h-9h am breakfast
- 12h-1h pm lunch
- 4h pm afternoon snack
- 7h-8h pm dinner
- 2 cheat meals per week (not entire days)

## Exercise

### Rules

- Pilates and Gym cannot cross
- Cardio can cross with Pilates

### Pilates

- monday, wednesday 6 pm (both)

### Gym

- tuesday at 6 pm, thursday at 6 pm and friday OR saturday at 6 pm as well
- 45-1h15 per session

### Cardio

- monday through friday at 7h am, saturday and sunday before 7 pm
- 140-150 heart-rate
  - treadmill 30-45 mins (default)
  - HIT: x secs/mins walking -> x secs/mins running -> repeat [20 mins]
  - bicycle ~1h
  - swimming

## Gamifying rules

- every of the following actions in every day accounts for at least 1 point
  - going to the gym
  - drinking a bottle of water
  - going to pilates class
  - waking up in time
  - getting to bedroom in time
  - getting to bed in time
  - every meal ate in time
  - every cardio session done
- each of the actions above should add up to some weekly score goal
  - max points means perfect week
  - threshold must be at 90% for a successful week
  - danger zone is under 60% of the weekly goal
- only actions that can overlap with working window are
  - drinking water
  - eating
