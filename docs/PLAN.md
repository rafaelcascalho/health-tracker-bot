## Point System

### Daily Points (max 14/day)

| Action | Points | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Wake up 7am | 1 | On time |
| Cardio | 1 | 30 min, 140-150 HR |
| Breakfast | 1 | 8-9am window |
| Lunch | 1 | 12-1pm window |
| Snack | 1 | 4pm |
| Dinner | 1 | 7-8pm window |
| Water Bottle 1 | 1 | Easy |
| Water Bottle 2 | 2 | Medium |
| Water Bottle 3 | 3 | Hard (before 8pm) |
| Bedroom | 1 | 10pm |
| Bed | 1 | 10:30pm, no phone |

### Weekly Exercise Points (max 5/week)

| Action | Points | Days |
|--------|--------|------|
| Pilates | 1 | Mon |
| Pilates | 1 | Wed |
| Gym | 1 | Tue |
| Gym | 1 | Thu |
| Gym | 1 | Fri OR Sat |

### Weekly Totals

| Category | Max Points |
|----------|------------|
| Daily actions (14 Ã— 7) | 98 |
| Weekly exercise | 5 |
| **TOTAL** | **103** |

### Thresholds

| Status | Points | Percentage |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Perfect Week | 103 | 100% |
| Successful Week | 93+ | 90%+ |
| Needs Improvement | 62-92 | 61-89% |
| Danger Zone | <62 | <60% |

### Cheat Meal Rules

- **Allowed:** 2 cheat meals per week (no penalty)
- **Penalty (progressive):**
  - 3rd cheat meal: -1 point
  - 4th cheat meal: -2 points
  - 5th cheat meal: -3 points
  - (continues: -n for n-2 cheat meal)

*Note: Penalties reduce weekly score but do NOT invalidate completed actions.*

## Meal Log Format

Simple chronological list. One line per meal.

```
[DATE] [MEAL]: [description]
```

**Examples:**

```
2026-01-25 B: scrambled eggs + toast + coffee
2026-01-25 L: grilled chicken + rice + salad
2026-01-25 S: greek yogurt + granola
2026-01-25 D: salmon + vegetables [CHEAT]
```

**Meal codes:** B = Breakfast, L = Lunch, S = Snack, D = Dinner

Mark cheat meals with `[CHEAT]` tag.
